Wellbeing

As much as social media can be a great way to keep in touch in times like this, it is important to remember that there can be some negative effects from overuse. These can include: Inadequacy about your life or appearance, Cyberbullying and Self-absorption.

Social media is a huge part of life during lockdown due to it being a key method of staying in-touch. Although you may want to stay online all day to stay busy, it is unhealthy to have greater than 5 hours screen time daily.
